Welcome to ReplaceMyFood

What is ReplaceMyFood? Imported food produces an estimated 4 times greater environmental impact than domestically produced food (Source: Center For Environmental Education). Local food also taste better as it is able to naturally ripen and supports your local economy. ReplaceMyFood gives you the place to advertise your locally sourced products as well as search for ways to replace your imported food.

Looking to replace your food? Go here: Buyer Home

Looking to list your product, market, or local business? Go here: Vendor Home

Looking to get into micro-farming? Contact us to find out what types of products people are looking for in your area! (We collect data about all of the queries people are making around you so that we can help local farmers to tailor their produce)
